Creating a webpack.config.js

Modern javascript apps often require node_modules which in turn require a bundler. Webpack is by far the most popular—it creates a single bundle.js in your index file in seconds, offering hot-module reloading and a variety of plugins from the webpack ecosystem. Painless.

Not so painless: generating a webpack.config.js. In a recent poll, developers aid it often takes 10-20 years to properly configure. Webpack wizard reduces that to mere seconds.
